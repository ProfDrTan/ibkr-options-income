Understanding MES — Start Here

📦 What is MES?
Micro E-mini S&P 500 futures. Tracks the S&P 500 index at $5 per point. If ES moves 10 points, you make or lose $50 per MES contract. It is the beginner-friendly version of the full ES contract which moves $50/point.
⏰ When Can You Trade?
MES trades 23 hours/day, Sunday 6pm ET to Friday 5pm ET. For Singapore/Malaysia traders, the prime window is 9pm–2am SGT when both Asian session and early US overnight session overlap with the most volume.
💰 How Much Do You Need?
IBKR requires approximately $1,500 margin per MES contract. Prof Dr Tan's recommended starting size: 1 contract with $10,000 in your account. Risk per trade: maximum $50-100 (10-20 ES points stop loss).
📈 The Basic Strategy
During Asian session, ES usually follows the direction set by Asian markets (Nikkei, Hang Seng). If Nikkei is up 1%+ and VIX is below 20, buy 1 MES at Asia open, target 10-15 points, stop at 8 points. Simple and mechanical.
🛡️ Risk Management
Never risk more than 1% of account per trade. With $50,000, that is $500 maximum risk = 100 ES points = never acceptable. Proper risk: $150 per trade = 30 ES points stop = 6 MES contracts maximum. Start with 1 and learn first.
🤝 Options + Futures Together
The combination is powerful. Options sell premium every Monday for weekly income (theta decay). Futures capture directional moves during Asian hours (delta gain). Together they generate income in ALL market conditions — trending OR sideways.
